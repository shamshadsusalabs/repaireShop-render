Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

LUXRE Workshop Management App ("we", "our", or "us") operates this application strictly as an internal enterprise tool for authorized workshop employees, mechanics, and managers.

1. Information We Collect

This App is an internal tool and collects information strictly necessary for the operation of the LUXRE auto repair workshop. We may collect:

  • Login Information: Employee name, email, and internal credentials.
  • Operational Data: Job cards, vehicle details, parts usage, and internal communications related to workshop operations.

2. How We Use the Information

The collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Managing daily auto repair operations and vehicle servicing.
  • Assigning jobs to mechanics and tracking progress.
  • Generating invoices and maintaining workshop inventory.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent any data to third parties. All data is securely stored and accessed only by authorized LUXRE management and staff for internal business operations.

4. Data Safety & App Permissions

Our app does not request access to personal contacts, precise location for tracking purposes, or personal financial information. The app only accesses network connectivity to sync data with our secure internal servers.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. As this is an internal enterprise application, any significant updates will be communicated directly to staff through internal channels.
